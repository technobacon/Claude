RETYPE-SPECIFIC NOTES FOR REVIEW & LAUNCH
-----------------------------------------
1. PERMISSION JUSTIFICATION (Google asks during review):
   - "<all_urls> content script": the product records typing on any site
     the user visits, locally, to recover it after data loss. No data
     leaves the device.
   - storage/unlimitedStorage: the typing archive itself.
   - activeTab: to know which site the popup is showing entries for and
     to insert text back into the page the user is looking at.
   - clipboardWrite: the "Copy" fallback when a field can't be found.

2. TEST THE DISASTER DEMO before recording screenshots: type into a form,
   close the tab, reopen, click Insert. That flow is the whole pitch.

3. LAUNCH ORDER (week 4 of the plan in ../research/IDEA_RESEARCH.md):
   a. Chrome Web Store listing live (keywords in STORE_LISTING.txt).
   b. Landing page with one SEO page per disaster ("session timed out",
      "lost my essay", "Workday application erased", "DS-160 timed out").
   c. Product Hunt launch (generic tools do well there).
   d. Helpful, non-spammy replies in daily "I lost everything I typed"
      threads on Reddit/X.

4. PRICING: $19 one-time, free tier = last 24h / 5 entries per view.
   Both knobs live in src/core/recorder.js (FREE_WINDOW_MS,
   FREE_ENTRY_LIMIT) and are covered by tests if you change them.
