Retype — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

The short version: Retype records what you type so YOU can get it back.
Everything stays on your computer. We never see it.

WHAT RETYPE STORES
- Text you type into ordinary text fields, text areas and rich-text editors
  on web pages, together with the page address, page title and a label for
  the field, so you can find and restore it later.
- Your settings (paused sites, retention limits, purchase status).

WHERE IT IS STORED
- Exclusively in your browser's local extension storage
  (chrome.storage.local) on your own device. Retype has no server. Nothing
  is uploaded, synced, transmitted or shared — with us or anyone else.

WHAT RETYPE NEVER RECORDS
- Password fields. Their value is never even read.
- Fields that look sensitive from their attributes or labels: credit card
  numbers, CVV/CVC, social security numbers, PINs, one-time codes and
  similar.
- Anything on sites you pause (toolbar popup → "Pause site").

YOUR CONTROLS
- Pause recording per site at any time.
- Delete any single entry, or everything at once ("Delete all recorded
  text" on the history page).
- Entries are pruned automatically by age and count (configurable).
- Uninstalling the extension deletes all stored data with it.

PAYMENTS
- The optional one-time purchase is processed by our payment provider
  (ExtensionPay / Stripe). We never see your card details. The extension
  stores only a yes/no purchase status.

ANALYTICS / TRACKING
- None. Retype contains no analytics, telemetry, ads or tracking of any
  kind.

CHANGES & CONTACT
- If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date changes and the new text
  ships with the extension update.
- Questions: contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
