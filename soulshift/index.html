How to Play

The Twist

You are a revenant soul. The body you wear is borrowed — it decays one point per action, and when it sloughs apart (or is destroyed) your bare soul spills out.

A bare soul flickers for a few turns (untouchable), then begins to fade. Step onto any corpse to possess it: its stats, speed and ability become yours. Your soul also strikes any body harder than its old owner could (+1 attack).

A soul's chilling touch slows the living — and any enemy weak enough (below 30% HP) can be seized alive.

Bodies you abandon remember their wounds and their rot. Fresh kills make the best skins.

The Goal

Descend 8 floors. The deep door is kept by the Warden. Kill it — or weaken it and possess it — and walk out wearing the dungeon's own face.

Shrines & Loot

Mend heals your body. Preserve resets its decay. Empower permanently sharpens your soul (+1 atk, +4 HP in every body, all run). All cost essence, which falls from kills. Unspent essence is banked when the run ends — more if you win.
