TabStash Privacy Policy
Last updated: [fill in the date you publish]

TabStash stores your saved workspaces (tab URLs, titles, and group
names) in Chrome's local storage on your device. This data never leaves
your device. It is not sent to any server, not shared with any third
party, and not accessible to the developer.

The extension does not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage data
of any kind.

Payment processing (if you purchase the Pro version) is handled by
ExtensionPay and Stripe. That transaction happens between you and those
services. TabStash itself only receives a true/false signal confirming
whether a purchase has been made.

The only permissions this extension requests are:
  - tabs: to read which URLs you have open when saving a workspace
  - tabGroups: to read and recreate tab group names and colors
  - storage: to save your workspaces locally on your device
